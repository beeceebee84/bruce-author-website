Book Description

In the quiet town of Eldermere, where the past lingers like morning fog, archivist Eleanor Voss discovers a collection of letters hidden in the walls of an abandoned house. The letters tell of a forgotten tragedy and a secret that the town has buried for generations.

As Eleanor pieces together the fragments of this decades-old mystery, she uncovers connections to her own family's history—connections that someone in Eldermere would rather keep hidden. When disturbing events begin to mirror those described in the letters, Eleanor realizes that some secrets aren't meant to stay buried.

"Whispers of the Forgotten" is a haunting tale of memory, redemption, and the echoes of the past that shape our present. With lyrical prose and masterful suspense, this novel will keep readers enthralled until its breathtaking conclusion.
