About Me

I write voice-driven fiction that’s intimate, raw, and honest.

To find the core of a genuine story, I embrace the unfiltered world. My work sits squarely between reality and fiction, inspired by unhinged travels that put me in the craziest places, meeting the most chaotic and inspiring people doing the wildest things. Every character, every extreme experience, every high-stakes moment on the page is rooted in a truth I had to live to find.

Burn Me Whole is the story I didn't know I needed to write, but the world needed me to share.
