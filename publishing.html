The Ink & Ember Story

Born from a frustration with traditional publishing models, Ink & Ember was created by authors, for authors. We understand the journey because we've lived it - the endless rejections, the confusing publishing jargon, the overwhelming technical requirements.

Our imprint BKR was founded on the belief that great stories deserve professional presentation without sacrificing creative control. We bridge the gap between traditional publishing and going it alone.

Every book we touch gets the same care and attention we'd give our own work. Because to us, publishing isn't just a service - it's a partnership.
