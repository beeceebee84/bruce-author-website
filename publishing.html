Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about process, files, and timelines.

What files should I send?
A Word doc (.docx) or Google Doc is ideal. If you only have a PDF, that’s fine too. Include your synopsis (optional) and any comp titles or goals.
How long does it take?
Typical turnaround: 30k ≈ 1–2 weeks, 60k ≈ 2–3 weeks, 100k ≈ 3–4 weeks. Rush options may be available — ask during your free consult.
What’s included in the assessment?
A written editorial letter, annotated manuscript comments, targeted sample rewrites, and a revision roadmap — plus follow-up support (7 / 14 / 21 days by tier).
Do you handle formatting or cover design?
Yes to interior formatting (KDP/ePub). Cover design is not included, but I can refer trusted designers or collaborate with yours.
Can I book outside Fiverr?
Absolutely. You can order directly here or choose Fiverr for buyer protection — the deliverables are the same.
